Stay consistent
Read this when you want to skip
The rules
1
Never miss twice
One miss is life. Two misses is the start of a spiral. This is the only rule that matters.
2
Downgrade, don't skip
Busy week? Mode B or C. You never fully stop — you just dial down.
3
Schedule it like a tute
Put sessions in your calendar with a specific time. Treat missing it like missing class.
4
Identity over motivation
You go because you're someone who trains — not because you feel like it today.
5
Remove friction
Pack your bag the night before. The harder it is to start, the more likely you bail.
Your known excuses
"I have exams / assignments"
Switch to Mode B. 2 sessions, 45 min. The gym improves exam performance. You have the time.
"Work picked up / I'm tired"
Mode B or C. Go anyway. Give yourself permission to leave after warm-up — you almost never will.
"I've already missed a few days"
This is the spiral talking. A 25-min Mode C session resets everything. Waiting until Monday is how a few days becomes 3 months.
"My shoulder is sore"
You study physio. Soreness is not a reason to skip — modify. Genuine injury → lower body only.
The reframe: Your goal is not perfect training weeks. Your goal is to never have two bad weeks in a row.
